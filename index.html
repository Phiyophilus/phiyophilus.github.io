About Kgothatso & Associates

Founded in 2010, Kgothatso & Associates has established itself as a leading provider of fraud and forensic investigation services in Southern Africa. Our team of certified forensic accountants, investigators, and digital forensics experts bring over 50 years of combined experience to every case.

We specialize in uncovering financial irregularities, investigating corporate fraud, and providing litigation support to legal professionals. Our meticulous approach ensures that every investigation is conducted with the highest standards of professionalism and integrity.
